Homemade Recipes

  • Procedure : In a bowl add cabbage, rice flour, corn flour, salt, garam masala,baking soda,mirchi powder,kothmeera and pudina Mix all the ingredients evenly. No water is required. Mix all the ingredients into a ball. Now take small part of the mixture and shape into bullets. In a kadai heat oil, enough for deep - fry. Now fry the bullets for 5-7 mins until they turn into golden brown Take them into a serving bowl and serve with ketchup.

  • procedure : In a jar add sweet corn, salt and green chillies. Blend them into a paste. Take the mixture into a bowl. Now add rice flour and mix. Excess water should be removed. In a kadai add oil and heat it. Now make the paste into small wada sizes. Fry them in oil till golden brown. Serve them with ketchup..
